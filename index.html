Lazy-Day Mug Cake

Craving something sweet but don’t want to deal with dishes or effort? This single-serve chocolate mug cake takes just five minutes to make. All you need is one mug, one spoon, and a few simple ingredients for a warm, gooey treat.

ingredients

  • 4 tbsp plain flour
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 tbsp milk (any kind)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil/melted butter
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1 tbsp chocolate chips or a few chunks (optional but highly recommended)

preparation

  1. In a large microwave-safe mug, mix the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add milk, melted butter/oil, and vanilla (if using). Stir until smooth and combined.
  3. Fold in chocolate chips if you’re going for extra gooey goodness.
  4. Microwave on high for 1 minute. If it looks too wet, add an extra 10–15 seconds.
  5. Let cool for a minute (seriously) before diving in.
